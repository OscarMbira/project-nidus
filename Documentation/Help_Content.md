# PM Simulator Help Content

## Quick Start Guide

### How to Start Your First Simulation

1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Take the skill assessment to get personalized recommendations
   - Set your learning goals

2. **Choose a Scenario**
   - Browse scenarios by difficulty, industry, or methodology
   - Read scenario descriptions and learning objectives
   - Start with a beginner scenario if you're new

3. **Select Your Role**
   - Choose a role that matches your learning goals
   - Each role offers different challenges and perspectives

4. **Run the Simulation**
   - Read each event carefully
   - Consider all response options
   - Make your decision
   - Review feedback to learn

## Common Questions

### Account & Subscription

**How do I upgrade my subscription?**
- Go to Subscription Management
- Click "Upgrade Plan"
- Select your desired tier
- Complete payment

**Can I cancel my subscription?**
- Yes, you can cancel anytime
- Access continues until the end of your billing period
- No refunds for partial periods

**What happens if my subscription expires?**
- You'll enter a 7-day grace period
- After grace period, access to premium features is restricted
- Your progress and data are preserved

### Simulations

**How long do simulations take?**
- Beginner: 30-60 minutes
- Intermediate: 1-2 hours
- Expert: 2-4+ hours

**Can I pause a simulation?**
- Yes, progress is saved automatically
- Resume from where you left off

**Can I retake scenarios?**
- Yes, unlimited retakes
- Improve your score and understanding
- Previous scores are saved

**What score do I need for a certificate?**
- Minimum 80% overall score
- Must complete all phases
- Professional tier or higher required

### Technical Issues

**Simulation won't load**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Scores not updating**
- Wait a few moments for processing
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support if issue persists

**Certificate not generating**
- Ensure score is 80% or higher
- Verify scenario completion
- Check subscription tier
- Contact support if needed

### Features

**How do I earn badges?**
- Complete specific achievements
- Reach milestones
- Maintain streaks
- Special accomplishments

**What are streaks?**
- Consecutive days of activity
- Earn bonus XP for streaks
- Maintain for 7+ days for maximum bonus

**How do leaderboards work?**
- Ranked by XP, score, or simulations completed
- Filter by role, industry, or time period
- Weekly and monthly resets

**Can I create custom scenarios?**
- Yes, with Professional tier
- Upload documents or paste text
- AI extracts scenario information
- Edit and validate before publishing

## Troubleshooting

### Performance Issues

**Page loads slowly**
- Check internet connection speed
- Close other browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Simulation is laggy**
- Reduce browser extensions
- Close other applications
- Check available memory
- Contact support if persistent

### Data Issues

**Progress not saving**
- Check internet connection
- Ensure you're logged in
- Refresh the page
- Contact support if data is lost

**Wrong scores displayed**
- Wait for processing to complete
- Refresh the page
- Check simulation completion status
- Contact support with details

## Best Practices

### Getting the Most from Simulations

1. **Read Carefully**: Take time to understand each event
2. **Think Strategically**: Consider long-term implications
3. **Learn from Feedback**: Review why decisions were good or bad
4. **Practice Regularly**: Consistent practice improves skills
5. **Challenge Yourself**: Gradually increase difficulty
6. **Join Community**: Learn from others' experiences

### Improving Your Scores

1. **Understand Context**: Consider project phase and situation
2. **Balance Priorities**: Balance timeline, budget, quality, and stakeholders
3. **Manage Risks**: Proactively address potential issues
4. **Communicate**: Keep stakeholders informed
5. **Support Team**: Make decisions that help your team succeed
