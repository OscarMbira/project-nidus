# Risk Management User Guide

## Overview

Risk Management is a universal module for identifying, assessing, mitigating, and monitoring risks across all project methodologies. This guide covers risk identification, risk assessment, mitigation planning, and risk monitoring.

## Accessing Risk Management

1. Navigate to your project from the **Projects** page
2. Click on **Risk Management** in the project navigation menu
3. View risks list, heat map, and dashboard

## Understanding Risks

### What is a Risk?

A risk is an uncertain event or condition that, if it occurs, has a positive or negative effect on project objectives.

### Risk Types

- **Threat**: Negative risk that could harm the project
- **Opportunity**: Positive risk that could benefit the project

### Risk Categories

- **Technical**: Technology-related risks
- **Financial**: Budget and cost risks
- **Schedule**: Time-related risks
- **Resource**: People and resource risks
- **External**: External factors (market, regulatory)
- **Organizational**: Internal organizational risks

## Creating Risks

### Creating a New Risk

1. Click **+ Create Risk** button
2. Fill in risk details:
   - **Risk Title** (required): Brief description
   - **Risk Code**: Unique identifier (auto-generated if blank)
   - **Description** (required): Detailed description
   - **Risk Type**: Threat or Opportunity
   - **Risk Category**: Technical, Financial, etc.
   - **Probability**: 1-5 scale (1=Very Low, 5=Very High)
   - **Impact**: 1-5 scale (1=Very Low, 5=Very High)
   - **Risk Owner**: Person responsible for managing risk
   - **Target Mitigation Date**: When to address risk
   - **Next Review Date**: When to review risk again
3. Click **Save**

### Risk Assessment

**Probability Assessment**:
- **1 - Very Low**: Unlikely to occur
- **2 - Low**: Possible but unlikely
- **3 - Medium**: Moderate likelihood
- **4 - High**: Likely to occur
- **5 - Very High**: Almost certain

**Impact Assessment**:
- **1 - Very Low**: Minimal impact
- **2 - Low**: Minor impact
- **3 - Medium**: Moderate impact
- **4 - High**: Significant impact
- **5 - Very High**: Critical impact

**Risk Score Calculation**:
- Risk Score = Probability × Impact
- Range: 1-25
- Higher score = higher priority

**Risk Level**:
- **Critical** (20-25): Immediate attention required
- **High** (12-19): Significant attention needed
- **Medium** (6-11): Monitor and manage
- **Low** (1-5): Accept and monitor

## Risk Heat Map

### Viewing Heat Map

1. Click **Heat Map** tab
2. View risk distribution:
   - X-axis: Probability (1-5)
   - Y-axis: Impact (1-5)
   - Color coding: Risk level
   - Risk count in each cell

### Interpreting Heat Map

- **Red Zone**: High probability, high impact (critical risks)
- **Orange Zone**: Medium-high probability/impact (high risks)
- **Yellow Zone**: Medium probability/impact (medium risks)
- **Green Zone**: Low probability/impact (low risks)

### Using Heat Map

- **Identify Critical Risks**: Focus on red zone
- **Prioritize Actions**: Address high-risk areas first
- **Monitor Trends**: Track risk movement over time
- **Communicate**: Visual tool for stakeholders

## Risk Status

### Status Types

1. **Identified**: Risk identified and logged
2. **Assessed**: Risk assessed (probability/impact)
3. **Mitigated**: Mitigation actions taken
4. **Monitored**: Ongoing monitoring
5. **Closed**: Risk no longer relevant
6. **Realized**: Risk has occurred (threat) or materialized (opportunity)

### Updating Status

1. Click on risk
2. Click **Update Status**
3. Select new status
4. Add status change comments
5. Save changes

## Mitigation Planning

### Response Strategies

**For Threats**:
- **Avoid**: Eliminate the threat
- **Transfer**: Shift risk to third party
- **Mitigate**: Reduce probability or impact
- **Accept**: Accept the risk

**For Opportunities**:
- **Exploit**: Ensure opportunity occurs
- **Share**: Partner to maximize benefit
- **Enhance**: Increase probability or impact
- **Accept**: Accept the opportunity

### Creating Mitigation Plans

1. Open risk details
2. Go to **Mitigation Plans** tab
3. Click **+ Add Mitigation**
4. Fill in mitigation details:
   - **Mitigation Action**: What will be done
   - **Description**: Detailed description
   - **Mitigation Type**: Preventive, Contingent, Corrective
   - **Assigned To**: Person responsible
   - **Planned Start Date**
   - **Planned Completion Date**
   - **Estimated Cost**: Cost of mitigation
5. Click **Save**

### Mitigation Status

- **Planned**: Mitigation planned
- **In Progress**: Mitigation being executed
- **Complete**: Mitigation completed
- **On Hold**: Mitigation paused

### Tracking Mitigation

1. Update mitigation status
2. Record actual dates
3. Log actual costs
4. Document results
5. Assess effectiveness

## Risk Monitoring

### Regular Reviews

1. Schedule regular risk reviews
2. Review all open risks
3. Update probability/impact if changed
4. Assess mitigation effectiveness
5. Identify new risks

### Review Checklist

- [ ] All risks reviewed
- [ ] Probability/impact updated
- [ ] Mitigation status checked
- [ ] New risks identified
- [ ] Risk register updated
- [ ] Stakeholders informed

### Risk Trends

Monitor risk trends:
- Number of risks over time
- Risk score trends
- Mitigation completion rates
- Realized risks

## Linking Risks

### Linking to Work Items

Risks can be linked to:
- **Tasks**: Related project tasks
- **Work Packages**: Structured PM work packages
- **Issues**: Related issues

### Adding Links

1. Open risk details
2. Go to **Links** section
3. Click **Link to Task/Work Package/Issue**
4. Select item from dropdown
5. Save link

1. **Regular Reviews**: Schedule regular reviews

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Risk Score Not Calculating**:
- Verify probability and impact set
- Check if values are 1-5
- Refresh page
- Contact administrator

**Heat Map Not Displaying**:
- Check if risks have probability/impact
- Verify risk data complete
- Refresh page
- Contact administrator

**Mitigation Not Saving**:
- Check all required fields
- Verify dates are valid
- Refresh page
- Contact administrator
