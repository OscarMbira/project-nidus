# Platform Getting Started Guide

**Project Nidus - Project Management Platform**

---

## Welcome to Platform

Welcome to Project Nidus Platform! This comprehensive project management tool helps you manage projects from startup to closure using structured methodologies. Whether you're a Project Manager, Team Lead, or Team Member, this guide will help you get started.

---

## Introduction

Project Nidus Platform is designed to support multiple project management methodologies including Structured PM, Scrum, and Kanban. The platform provides:

- **Structured Project Workflows** - Follow proven methodologies for consistent delivery
- **Role-Based Dashboards** - Customized views for different user roles
- **Task Management** - Create, assign, and track tasks with dependencies
- **Resource Allocation** - Manage team members and resources efficiently
- **Risk & Issue Management** - Proactively identify and manage project risks
- **Reporting & Analytics** - Generate comprehensive reports and insights
- **Team Collaboration** - Centralized communication and collaboration tools

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Navigate to the Platform homepage
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Verify your email address (check your inbox for verification link)
5. Complete your profile setup

### Your First Login

1. Go to the Platform login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Login"**
4. You'll be taken to your dashboard based on your role

## Understanding Your Role

Project Nidus supports different user roles, each with specific permissions and dashboards:

### Project Manager
- Create and manage projects
- Assign tasks and resources
- Monitor project progress
- Generate reports
- Manage risks and issues

### Team Lead
- Manage team tasks
- Plan sprints (Scrum)
- Manage Kanban boards
- Review team performance
- Coordinate team activities

### Team Member
- View assigned tasks
- Update task status
- Log time spent
- Collaborate with team
- View project information

## Your First Project

### Creating a New Project

1. Navigate to **Projects** from the main menu
2. Click **"Create New Project"**
3. Enter project details:
   - **Project Name**: Clear, descriptive name
   - **Description**: Project scope and objectives
   - **Start Date**: Project kickoff date
   - **End Date**: Target completion date (optional)
   - **Methodology**: Choose from Structured PM, Scrum, or Kanban
4. Click **"Create"**

### Choosing a Methodology

**Structured PM**
- Best for large, complex projects
- Formal stage gates and approvals
- Comprehensive documentation
- Multiple process groups

**Scrum**
- Best for iterative, agile projects
- Sprints and sprint planning
- Product backlog management
- Scrum events (daily standups, reviews)

**Kanban**
- Best for continuous workflow
- Visual board management
- Work-in-progress limits
- Continuous delivery
