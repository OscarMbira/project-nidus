## Training Schedule

Training sessions should be scheduled as follows:

- **Administrator Training**: 8 hours (full day)
- **Project Manager Training**: 6 hours (full day)
- **Team Lead Training**: 4 hours (half day)
- **Team Member Training**: 2 hours (morning/afternoon session)

## Training Assessments

Assessments should be created for each role to validate:
1. Understanding of core concepts
2. Ability to perform key tasks
3. Knowledge of best practices

## Training Feedback

Feedback forms should be collected after each training session to:
1. Measure training effectiveness
2. Identify areas for improvement
3. Gather suggestions for future training
