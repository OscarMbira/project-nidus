## Introduction

Project Nidus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our project management application and services.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password
- **Profile Information**: Profile picture, job title, organization
- **Content**: Projects, tasks, comments, files, and other content you create
- **Communication**: Messages, support requests, feedback

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Data**: How you use the application, features accessed, time spent
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed
- **Cookies**: Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Cookie Policy)

### Information from Third Parties
- **SSO Providers**: If you sign in using SSO (Single Sign-On)
- **Integrated Services**: Data from integrated services (Jira, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace)

- **Account Management**: Create and manage your account

- **Customer Support**: Respond to your requests and provide support

- **Analytics**: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

- **Compliance**: Comply with legal obligations and regulations

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
We retain your information as long as your account is active and as needed to provide services.

### Inactive Accounts
We retain information for inactive accounts for 90 days after account deactivation.

### Legal Requirements
We may retain information longer as required by law, legal obligations, or legitimate business interests:
- **Audit Logs**: Retained for 7 years for compliance
- **Financial Records**: Retained as required by financial regulations
- **Legal Holds**: Retained during legal proceedings

### Data Deletion
You can request account deletion at any time. We will delete your information within 30 days of your request, subject to legal retention requirements.

## Your Rights

### Access
You have the right to access your personal information and receive a copy of your data.

### Rectification
You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

### Erasure
You have the right to request deletion of your personal information ("right to be forgotten").

### Data Portability
You have the right to receive your data in a machine-readable format.

### Objection
You have the right to object to processing of your personal information for certain purposes.

### Restriction
You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal information.

### Withdraw Consent
You have the right to withdraw consent at any time (if processing is based on consent).

### Exercise Your Rights
To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@projectnidus.com or use the Privacy Center in the application.

## Data Security

### Security Measures
We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption**: Encrypt data at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3, AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Implement access controls and authentication
- **Monitoring**: Monitor systems for security threats
- **Regular Audits**: Conduct regular security audits

### Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR.
